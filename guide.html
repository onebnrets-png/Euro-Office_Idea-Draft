📘 Euro-Office User Guide

EU Intervention Logic AI Assistant

Version 1.1.0 — March 2026

1 Registration & Login

1.1 Registration

  1. Open your browser and navigate to https://euro-office.net
  2. Click "Register here" at the bottom of the login form
  3. Enter your e-mail address (must be valid) and password (minimum 6 characters)
  4. Click "Register"
  5. Check your inbox for a confirmation email and click the activation link
  6. Return to Euro-Office and log in with your credentials

1.2 Login

  1. Enter your e-mail address and password
  2. Click "Login"
  3. If two-factor authentication (2FA) is enabled, enter the code from your authenticator app
  4. You will be taken to the Home Screen

1.3 Language Selection

In the top-right corner, click SI or EN to switch both the interface language and the AI content generation language. The active language is highlighted in blue.

5 AI Generation Modes

5.1 Section-Level Generation

At the top of each section, you will find three generation buttons:

ModeIconWhat it does
Fill📝Fills only empty fields. Keeps your existing content untouched.
Enhance✨Deepens existing content. Adds citations, EU terminology, evidence and data.
Regenerate🔄Creates completely new content from scratch. Replaces existing data.

5.2 Field-Level AI Assistant NEW

Every text field in Euro-Office now has a dedicated AI Assistant button (✨ icon) that appears when you hover over the field, in the top-right corner of the text area. This opens a contextual popup where you can:

  1. See the current field content — the popup shows what's already in the field
  2. Enter specific instructions — tell the AI exactly what you want (e.g., "add ISO references", "make it more formal", "expand with concrete examples from Western Balkans")
  3. Generate / Regenerate — AI generates content using your instructions, the current field value, full project context, knowledge base documents, and all configured rules
  4. Preview the result — review the generated content before accepting
  5. Accept or Regenerate — accept the result to replace the field content, or regenerate with modified instructions
💡 Pro Tip: The field-level AI Assistant is far more powerful than section-level generation because you can give precise, contextual instructions for each individual field. Use it to fine-tune specific paragraphs, add particular references, or adjust tone and formality.

5.3 Evidence-Based Content & Citations NEW

Euro-Office enforces strict academic rigor in all generated content:

  • Mandatory empirical data — every analytical paragraph must contain at least one verified data point with source attribution
  • APA-style citations — author surnames, publication year, and at least 30% author-named citations throughout the text
  • Zero-hallucination policy — the AI is forbidden from fabricating sources, URLs, authors, or statistics. If data cannot be verified, it is clearly stated.
  • Source hierarchy — local/regional data takes priority, followed by Eurostat, OECD, EC, and scientific publications
💡 Recommended Workflow: Write your core problem title and a few sentences of description manually. Then click Regenerate to let AI generate the full analysis with evidence. Review the output, then use Enhance to further deepen with additional citations. Use the AI Assistant on individual fields to fine-tune specific areas.

6 Problem Analysis

This is the foundation of your entire project. Every cause identified here must later be addressed by an activity, and every consequence must connect to an impact.

6.1 Core Problem

Enter a concise Title and a detailed Description with quantitative evidence. Even a few sentences are enough to start — AI will expand and enrich it with citations from real sources.

6.2 Causes (minimum 4)

Click "+ Add Cause" or let AI generate them. Causes should be ordered from root/structural causes first to proximate causes second. Each cause gets a title and a description with evidence-based citations.

6.3 Consequences (minimum 4)

Click "+ Add Consequence" or let AI generate them. Consequences should show the escalation chain: local → regional → national → EU-level policy impact.

6.4 Inline Visualizations

After generation, a "Generate visualizations" button may appear below descriptions containing empirical data. Click it to create charts (bar, gauge, donut) that visualize statistics mentioned in the text.

7 Project Idea

This chapter translates the problem analysis into a proposed intervention.

7.1 Project Title & Acronym

Title: A concise noun phrase (30–200 characters). Must NOT contain the acronym or be a full sentence. Think of it as a project brand.

Acronym: 3–8 uppercase letters derived from the title keywords (e.g., GREENTRANS, DIGI-CRAFT). Must be pronounceable.

7.2 Main Aim

One comprehensive sentence starting with an infinitive verb (e.g., "To develop a cross-border digital platform that strengthens educational resilience...").

7.3 State of the Art

AI generates a thorough analysis referencing at least 3 real existing EU projects and studies, describing how your project capitalises on past results and avoids duplication (synergies and capitalisation).

7.4 Proposed Solution

A detailed description including: introductory overview paragraph, phase-by-phase breakdown, and a mandatory DNSH (Do No Significant Harm) compliance statement.

7.5 EU Policies

At least 3 relevant EU policies with specific alignment descriptions. AI generates them with real, verifiable policy references.

7.6 Readiness Levels

8 Objectives

8.1 General Objectives (3–5)

Each starts with an infinitive verb and connects to broader EU goals. Each has a title, description, and KPI indicator field.

8.2 Specific Objectives (5–7)

Follow the S.M.A.R.T. method: Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound. Each has a measurable KPI indicator with quantitative targets, baselines, and timeframes.

💡 Example S.M.A.R.T. Indicator: "Increase the percentage of pilot school students completing the academic year from 82% to 93% by M24, verified by school administration records across 5 partner regions."

9 Activities (Work Packages)

This is the most complex section. AI generates the full work plan in a two-phase approach.

9.1 Work Package Structure

PositionWP TypeDuration
WP1Foundational / Analytical (incl. Capitalisation & Synergies)Specific phase
WP2–WP(N-2)Content / Thematic WPs in logical sequenceSequential with overlaps
WP(N-1)Dissemination, Communication & ExploitationFull duration (M1–end)
WP(N)Project Management & CoordinationFull duration (M1–end)

9.2 Each Work Package Contains

Tasks (2–5) with title, description, start/end dates, and task dependencies (FS, SS, FF, SF). Milestones (1–2) as completion checkpoints. Deliverables (1–3) with binary verifiable indicators (Lump Sum compliant).

9.3 Two-Phase AI Generation

  1. Phase 1 — Scaffold: AI generates WP titles and date ranges for the entire project
  2. Phase 2 — Detail: AI generates each WP individually with full tasks, milestones, deliverables, and cross-WP dependencies

A progress indicator shows which WP is being generated. This takes 1–3 minutes for 6–10 WPs — this is normal.

💡 Temporal Integrity: Euro-Office automatically enforces that NO task, milestone, or deliverable falls outside the project start/end dates. Out-of-range dates are automatically corrected.

12 Expected Results & KERs

12.1 Outputs (5–8)

Direct, tangible deliverables. Each has a result-oriented noun phrase title, description, and binary verifiable indicator.

12.2 Outcomes (4–6)

Medium-term changes with measurable indicators, quantitative targets, and measurement methods.

12.3 Impacts (3–5)

Long-term strategic shifts categorised into Key Impact Pathways (KIP): Scientific, Societal, Economic.

12.4 Key Exploitable Results — KERs (4–6)

Specific assets with exploitation strategies covering three sustainability pillars: Financial, Institutional, and Political/Regulatory.

💡 Composite Generation: Clicking Regenerate on Expected Results generates Outputs, Outcomes, Impacts, and Risks together, ensuring cross-reference consistency.

5.2 AI Asistent na ravni polja NOVO

Vsako besedilno polje v Euro-Office ima zdaj svoj gumb AI Asistenta (ikona ✨), ki se prikaže, ko se z miško pomaknete nad polje, v zgornjem desnem kotu besedilnega polja. Ta odpre kontekstualno okno, kjer lahko:

  1. Vidite trenutno vsebino polja — okno prikaže, kaj je že v polju
  2. Vnesete specifična navodila — povejte AI natančno, kaj želite (npr. "dodaj ISO reference", "napiši bolj formalno", "razširi s konkretnimi primeri iz Zahodnega Balkana")
  3. Generirajte / Regenerirajte — AI generira vsebino z vašimi navodili, trenutno vrednostjo polja, celotnim kontekstom projekta, dokumenti baze znanja in vsemi konfiguriranimi pravili
  4. Predogled rezultata — preglejte generirano vsebino pred sprejetjem
  5. Sprejmite ali regenerirajte — sprejmite rezultat za zamenjavo vsebine polja ali regenerirajte s spremenjenimi navodili
💡 Nasvet: AI Asistent na ravni polja je veliko močnejši od generiranja na ravni sekcije, ker lahko daste natančna, kontekstualna navodila za vsako posamezno polje. Uporabite ga za natančno uravnavanje posameznih odstavkov, dodajanje specifičnih referenc ali prilagajanje tona.

5.3 Vsebina na podlagi dokazov in citati NOVO

Euro-Office uveljavlja strogo akademsko strogost v vseh generiranih vsebinah:

  • Obvezni empirični podatki — vsak analitični odstavek mora vsebovati vsaj eno preverjeno podatkovno točko z navedbo vira
  • Citiranje po APA standardu — priimki avtorjev, leto objave in vsaj 30% poimensko navedenih citatov
  • Politika ničelnih halucinacij — AI-ju je prepovedano izmišljevati vire, URL-je, avtorje ali statistike. Če podatkov ni mogoče preveriti, je to jasno navedeno.
  • Hierarhija virov — lokalni/regionalni podatki imajo prednost, sledijo Eurostat, OECD, EK in znanstvene publikacije
💡 Priporočen potek dela: Napišite naslov osrednjega problema in nekaj stavkov opisa ročno. Nato kliknite Regeneriraj za celotno analizo z dokazi. Preglejte rezultat, nato uporabite Izboljšaj za poglobitev z dodatnimi citati. Uporabite AI Asistenta na posameznih poljih za natančno uravnavanje.

6 Analiza problema

To je temelj vašega celotnega projekta. Vsak identificiran vzrok mora biti pozneje obravnavan z aktivnostjo, vsaka posledica pa se mora povezati z vplivom.

6.1 Osrednji problem

Vnesite jedrnat Naslov in podroben Opis s kvantitativnimi dokazi. Tudi nekaj stavkov je dovolj za začetek — AI bo razširil in obogatil z citati iz resničnih virov.

6.2 Vzroki (najmanj 4)

Kliknite "+ Dodaj vzrok" ali pustite AI, da jih generira. Vzroki naj bodo urejeni od korenskih/strukturnih najprej do neposrednih vzrokov.

6.3 Posledice (najmanj 4)

7 Projektna ideja

To poglavje prevede analizo problema v predlagano intervencijo.

7.1 Naslov projekta in akronim

Naslov: Jedrnata imenska fraza (30–200 znakov). NE sme vsebovati akronima ali biti celoten stavek.

Akronim: 3–8 velikih črk iz ključnih besed naslova (npr. GREENTRANS, DIGI-CRAFT).

7.2 Glavni cilj

En izčrpen stavek, ki se začne z nedoločnikom (npr. "Razviti čezmejno digitalno platformo, ki krepi izobraževalno odpornost...").

7.4 Predlagana rešitev
